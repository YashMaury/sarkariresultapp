Q 2. Can I get Government Job after 10th?

ᐅ Yes. All Govt Organizations and Govt Companies require Inter/Matric/ 10th class pass students. After 10th pass students get Government job on Govt Jobs from Railway, Public Sector Companies, SSC, HSSC, UPSC, Banking, Police Departments, Government Ministry departments, Government Universities, etc.

Q 3. Which job is best for 12th pass?

ᐅ Government Jobs are the most secure and highest-paying job for 12th pass students. Best Job sector for 12th Pass: (i)✔️ Railway » Group C posts, Group D posts, Trackman, Helper, Assistant Points Man, Safaiwala / Safaiwali, Gunman, Peon, Clerk, Ticket collector, etc. (ii) ✔️ Banking Sector » Clerk, Customer Support, Assistant. (iii)✔️ Defense / Police » Constable, Forestman, Drivers, Fireman, etc.

Q 4. What is the starting salary for the 12th pass?

ᐅ There are some good jobs in the Govt sector which pay a very good salary to 12th pass students. For Example. Forest Guard, Police Constable, SSC CHSL, MTS, DEO, Clerical, Peon, Process Server, and LDC posts pay between 15000 to 45000 Rupees Per Month.
